New York City

New York City is one of the most popular cities on the East Coast. The large city is home to many other notable cities and places. The city's five different sections ae Manhattan, Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island. Each division has a different lifestyle that makes the people who live there a New Yorker. New York City is one of the most notable and populous cities in the entire world because of its businuses and cultures that shape the city to what it is today.

Miami

Miami is a very favored city for many who seek warm weather and futuristic acitecture. This city is located in Southeas Florida and is home to many different ports and so many beaches to explore. The most popular of those two is the Port of Miami and Miami Beach which many enjoy going to for vacations down south.

Philadelphia

Philadelphia is a city full of history. This city is located in Southeast Pennsylvania and is home to cold weather and a very metropolitain lifestyle. Philadelphia is also where the declaration fo Independence was signed and the birthplace of the Industria Revolution. People often travel here to see this city's history and how the city functions as well as its most notable monuments that make this city so special.
